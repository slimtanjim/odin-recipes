Chili Ramen

Description:

Sometimes you want your ramen to have a kick, a flare, a dash of artisan touch. The perfect dish, a chef's kiss worthy nutrient source does not have to be scavenged so far and wide. One can have it in mere seconds. Just dump a whole can if chili in it :)
done.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack of Ramen
  • 1 can of Chili
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 stem of spring onion

Direction:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add dry noodles into the pot, breaking up as soften.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mix in the seasonings pack.
  5. Wait for 1 minute for the ramen to soak in the flavor
  6. Pour in the entire can of Chili and stir occasionally on medium heat for 2 minutes
  7. Pour in to a bowl and garnish it with a pinch of chopped spring onion
