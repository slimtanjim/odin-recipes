Ramac and Cheese

Description:

Vanilla Ramen is cool. Spiced up Ramen is even cooler. But what if you want something right in the middle. With a clogged mind, why not also clog up your heart. With this fantastic all american all creamy mac and cheese effused ramen.

Ingredients:

  • Pack of Ramen noodle
  • American cheese (sliced or shedded)
  • 3 cups of water

Direction:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add dry noodles into the pot, breaking up as soften.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mix in the seasonings pack.
  5. Transfer into the serving bowl and sprinkle in the shredded cheese (slab on the slices if sliced cheese, gently )
  6. Stir until cheese it fully devolved leaving the noodles thicc and creamy
