Ramen Sandwich

Description:

One cheatcode to making ramen is actully not making it at all. The hard crispy noodles that are packed are alreadyy edible as is; no cookery required. What is a good was to consume this new found food hack? well why not make a sandwich out of it :)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Ramen pack
  • 1 slick of american cheese(or any cheese you like)
  • Some slices of bologna

Direction:

  1. Carefully open the ramen pack and carefully take out the noodles from inside
  2. With your thumb pull apart the noodles into 2 halves. Each will be the buns of our sandwich
  3. Start stacking your sandwitch which ever way you like
  4. Place onto a thich papaer plate and enjoy
